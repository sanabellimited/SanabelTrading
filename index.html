A brief summary of our company:

SANABEL is an import and export company, it's a company registered with the Pakistani authorities in Islamabad, and a member of the chamber of commerce in the Pakistani capital.The company opens the window of import to Pakistan when the price is favorable and appropriate for the purchasing of the Pakistanis, or that the goods are desirable and required in the market and to the merchants, or it is required as a raw material for manufacturing. The founders of the company also have long experience in the Pakistani market and in the fields of commercial activities, and they have varied and successful experiences. It also aims to attract Arab investors and others who wished to establish successful and profitable investment projects for them in Pakistan. In addition, the company’s personnel have extensive relationships and a large communication network with Pakistani factory owners and export companies. Therefore, we are pleased to provide consulting services to customers in general for a very reasonable fee for each required task or desired mission.
